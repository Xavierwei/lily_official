exchange
process

Log in at official website point exchange page

Call member service hotline 4008-91-9135 to exchange

 

Choose the gift you like, choose the store you would like to pick up, submit exchange order.

Order submitted successfully. The system send verify code for picking up the gift on you cell phone.

The gift will be delivered to your designated store in about 10 working days.

The store will call to notify you to pick up your gift with your verify code within one months.

You go to the store to pick up the gift with the verify code.

You haven’t picked up within one month, you will not be qualified to pick up the gift, and your points will not be returned.

The exchange is over.
